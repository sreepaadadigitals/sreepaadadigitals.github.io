What we offer

Flex Design & Printings

Flex is a durable, fade-resistant and tear resistant material which can be used for outdoor as well as indoor signage. These are mounted using wooden/metallic frames or tied up

Vinyl

This product has custom print on one side and self adhesive sticker on the other side which can be used to apply the vinyl on different surfaces such as glass, walls, foam boards, etc.

Backlit Boards

This is a slim backlit board with lighting and a snap on Aluminium Frame. The image is digitally printed on a Translite Film in high resolution. This is a replaceable film.

Standee

We provide aluminium frame roll up standees with printed media. Standees come with digital print, aluminium rods, stand, and cover.And its installation services.

Sun Board

Sun board or Foam board is a light, easily cut sheet material used for the mounting of vinyl prints. It is usually used in signages, in-store displays & indoor branding.

Visting Cards

We design visting cards and business cards. Card bearing business inforamtion about company or individual information, contact information.
